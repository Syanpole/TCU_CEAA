# Quick Navigation Guide - TCU-CEAA Portal

## 🗺️ Where to Find the New Pages

```
TCU-CEAA Portal Landing Page
│
└── Footer Section (Bottom of Page)
    │
    └── Support Column
        ├── 📚 FAQ ──────────────────────> FAQ Page
        ├── 💬 Help Center ──────────────> Help Center Page
        ├── 🔒 Privacy Policy ───────────> Privacy Policy Page
        └── 📋 Terms of Service ─────────> Terms of Service Page
```

---

## 🎯 Page Access Flow

### From Landing Page:
1. Scroll down to footer
2. Look for **"Support"** section (rightmost column)
3. Click any of the four options
4. Page will open with full content
5. Click **"← Back to Home"** or click the **TCU-CEAA Portal** logo to return

## 🔄 Navigation Options

From any informational page, you can:

1. **Click "← Back to Home"** button (top right)
2. **Click "TCU-CEAA Portal"** logo/title (top left)
3. Use browser back button
4. Click scroll-to-top button to go to page top
